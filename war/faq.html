FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

What is The House all about ?

The house of change is our initiative to bring about a change in the mindset of the people towards the betterment of our society and country as a whole . Its motto is to create a sense of awareness among the people,about their responsibilities,duties towards their society . Its all about bringing realization and introspection among our fellow citizens .

What is Hall of Shame,Change,Fame ?

The hall of shame is a depiction of pictures of citizens involved in unsocial activities and these pictures can be viewed by other users on a shared page . Here people can Upload images of those who disobey laws or are involved in inhuman activities in our country . The Hall of Change provides an opportunity for those whose pictures have been uploaded to hall of shame,if they want to become a better human being . The Hall of Fame includes recognising and appreciating welfare work for the society,promoting positivity among all .

Is Registration of House Of Change web application free ?

As our motto is to create the change in soiety,thus "The House Of Change " web application is absolutely free to all the users,and will always be .

How does House of change works ?

    House of change is basically a web application developed for the welfare of the society.It is broadly categorized into three
    1)House of shame
    2)House of change
    3)House of fame ..
  • ->House of shame - Whenever u see a person doing any harm to the society u have to just click the photograph of the incident and post it to our house of shame.The photograph after passing through screening phase would then be uploaded on the house of shame.House of shame will list the photographs of all such shameful incidents with a social issue being highlighted in every photograph.All the users can like the pic,comment or share those photographs to raise voice against them .
  • ->House of change -The person who claims for being a part of any shameful incident listed in house of shame,and wants to apologize for that,then he 'll have to sign a petition for that.The signed petition will then be approved /disapproved by the admins of the application . After which that photograph would be removed from house of shame and instead would be posted in house of change ;to highlight the change .
  • ->House of fame -House of fame will list All those users who are doing wonders,leaving no stone unturned to create social reformity .. The users can only view this house .. to get soe kind of inspiration from them ..

Who can upload images ?

All registered users have the right to upload images on the hall of shame.For this,the users need to sign up on the login page.Once registered,users can login in their successive visits to the page .

If i am not a registered user,what can i do on the website ?

All the users who havn 't registered can just view the gallery in Hall of shame,Change and Fame . They dont have the access to like,or comment or upload images on any page .

How to upload any pic in the Hall of shame ?

To upload pictures on our website,you have to be a registered user.Click on the Upload button on the screen and along with the image,add details like description,date,place and time .

Can i also upload content on Hall of Fame or Hall of Change ?

No,its not possible for the registered users to upload images on Hall of Change or Fame because that is the task of the monitoring team . They will shift the images of those people who wish to change to become better by realiztion from Hall of Shame to Hall of Change . Similarly,they will manage the working of the Hall of Fame and the content associated with it .

What should i do to sign up ?

To sign up for the first time,you need to visit the login page,fill in ur name,password and an emailid to register yourself .

Can I share the picture on social media ?

Yes,you can.Every image has a Share option that can help to share the image on social media like facebook or twitter .

What is the concept of petition letter in the Hall of Change ?

The petition letter is a way that helps people who wish to get themselves removed from the Hall of shame . By signing the petition letter,the person takes a genuine promise to improve as a human being .
